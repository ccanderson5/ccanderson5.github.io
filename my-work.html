My Work

I have a lot of hobbies which leads to a lot of projects; some of which get finished, others do not. I made a Youtube channel when I was nine and my goal was to make videos of me playing games just like the people I watched on Youtube. Over time that spread to me making and uploading whatever I thought was a fun display of work.

The Box

My friends and I loved to try to make movies when we were in middle and high school, so we eventually ended up uploading one of them in 2019. The Box went on to take home Best Picture at the Oscars that year. It recently celebrated it's 5th anniversary in August 2024.

Calculated By Dennis

This is the most popular video on my channel by a large margin. It also makes the least sense. When I was in high school, I was really into professional eSports, I even went to two tournaments in 2018. 2019 came along and my favorite team had just qualified for a major event, so I made a song about how their worst player was actually the only reason they qualified. I wrote, recorded, and uploaded it in 4 hours, and you can definitely tell.

MGFFL

In 2020, I had a lot of free time during online school and I started a little recap show that was short lived about the fantasy football league my friends and I started back in 2017. Even though I only did a couple videos, I still thought it was a fun project I've considered returning to this NFL season if time permits. You can learn more about my sports interests on my About Me page.

Computer Generated Art

In freshman year of high school, I took Computer Generated Art with a friend of mine. It had four main projects: a magazine cover, an animation, a 3D model, and a set of emojis. My personal favorite was the emoji project as I themed mine around Yung Gravy. One of the emojis I created has become my go to profile picture.

COMARTS 155 Website

Last year, I took COMARTS 155 with Eric Hoyt. Worth every second. We had four major projects exploring different media types and creating our own. The first project was photography, then podcast, then a website, and finally a group video. I feel it's only right to share my website from the class on this site.
